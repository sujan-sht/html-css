Welcome To Hotel Himalaya

Hotel Himalaya is a Tibetan themed boutique hotel in Bhaktapur offering the best in modern amenities with traditional Nepalese hospitality. Located along the prestigious “embassy road” Hotel Himalaya is just 10 minutes drive from the city centre, in close proximity to major embassies, businesses and within easy access to major tourist attractions.
